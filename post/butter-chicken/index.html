Butter Chicken

45 minutes
 indian 

Optional

  • Soak cashews in water
  • Marinade chicken

Ingredients

  • Oil (Olive/Avocado/Canola) (2-3 Tbsp)

Tier 1

  • Cumin Seeds (1 Tbsp)
  • Onion (2)
  • Garlic (6 cloves)
  • Ginger (3 Tbsp)
  • Cashews (12)
  • Salt to taste (½ - 1 Tbsp)

Tier 2

  • Tomatoes (2)
  • Canned Tomato Paste (1)
  • Sugar (2 Tbsp)
  • Ground Cumin (1 Tbsp)
  • Ground Coriander (1 Tbsp)
  • Turmeric (1 Tbsp)
  • Kashmiri Red Chilli (1 Tbsp)
  • Ground Red Chilli (1 Tbsp)

Tier 3

  • Chicken Breast Cubed (2-3 Chicken Breast)

Tier 4

  • Whipping Cream (3 Tbsp)
  • Butter (2 Tbsp)
  • Fenugreek Leaves (1 Tbsp)

Prep

  • Tier 1

    • Finely Chop Onions
    • Mince Ginger and Garlic

  • Tier 2

    • Dice Tomatoes

  • Tier 3

    • Dice Chicken into small cubes

Process

  1. Heat up oil
  2. Throw in Tier 1a ~ 2 minute
  3. Throw in Tier 1b ~ 5 minutes
  4. Throw in Tier 2 and let it cook ~ 15 minutes
  5. Let it cool down for ~ 5-10 minutes
  6. While it is cooling down, prep Tier 3 ~ 5 minutes
  7. Once step 5 is done being cooled, blend it using a blender. Hand blender can also be utilized for this. ~ 6 minutes
  8. Once done blending, use fine mesh strainer and strain the gravy.
  9. Throw in Tier 3 in the same pot/pan which was used earlier. ~ 7 minutes
  10. Throw in strained gravy and cook ~ 3 minutes
  11. Throw in Tier 4, stir well ~ 1 minute
    1. Turn off the stove
    2. let it cool down
